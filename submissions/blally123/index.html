How I got into Yo-Yoing

When I was young I had played with a yo-yo here and there but never actually gained any skill at it, until my freshmen year of college. I went home for springbreak to stay with my parents and happend upon my old yo-yo -- which I hadn't touched in years -- with my newfound young-adult levels of coordination I quickly picked up on many tricks. I soon found that getting out of bed and throwing a few yo-yo tricks was a great way to wake myself up in the morning, after those late nights of writing long lab reports. To this day I have been actively yo-yoing for about 5 months now, so it didn't take long for me to pick it up at all.

Why should anyone Yo-Yo?

Yo-Yoing serves as a means of physical challenge without the phyical toll or danger usually associated with that -- atleast most of the time anyways -- for anybody who enjoys challenging themselves on the go with very little downtime, the Yo-Yo is a good choice. I'm not going to tell anybody that they should or should not Yo-Yo, but if you asked me I would tell you that you should atleast give it a try, regardless of how dumb you may thing it is you can deny the satisfaction associated with getting the object to return to your hand.

My Yo-Yo's

I currently have about 13 or so yo-yos, which to somebody without any experience with yo-yo's may sound like large number to have, but in reality a majority of people who yo-yo have quite a large collection, as each yo-yo is slightly different than one another. I find them much more novel to collect than things such as trading cards, as each individual yo-yo can be played with and each yo-yo plays differently from one another.

My Future as a Yo-Yo Dude

I want it to be clear that I do not ever intend on joining the circus or anything, but for the sake of having it as a hobby, I expect for my yo-yo throwing to continue for years to come. I enjoy yo-yoing for the challenge involved in learning "that next trick", for the sheer difficulty involved in some yo-yo tricks it gives me great satisfaction to land my most difficult tricks cleanly.
